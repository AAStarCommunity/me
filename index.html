About Me

I am an idealistic life hacker and community contributor, working on Digital Public Goods and Society Enhancement powered by Blockchain and AI.

I am currently a PhD Candidate in Digital Innovation at Chiang Mai University, Thailand. My research primarily focuses on Blockchain Account Security, Ethereum Account Abstraction (ERC-4337), and decentralized human-computer interaction paradigms.

I am also the founder of the AAStar Community and the Chiang Mai University Blockchain Association (CMUBA), dedicated to promoting blockchain education, open-source development, and pushing the boundaries of Web3 accessibility and mass adoption.
